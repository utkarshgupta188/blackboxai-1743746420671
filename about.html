Our Story

From humble beginnings to becoming a trusted partner for entrepreneurs worldwide.

Our Journey

2020 - Foundation

Founded in Bangalore with a team of 3 passionate entrepreneurs who saw the need for better business support systems.

2021 - First 100 Clients

Helped our first 100 clients achieve their business goals, specializing in tech startups and retail businesses.

2022 - Expansion

Opened offices in Mumbai and Delhi, expanding our team to 25 business consultants and strategists.

2023 - Global Reach

Partnered with international firms to help Indian businesses expand globally and bring foreign investment.
